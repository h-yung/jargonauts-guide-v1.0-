About

Business jargon is like learning a dialect that borrows from many industries, but you don't need an AI or some SaaS product that begins with a G to write your emails with the veneer of professional legitimacy. Watch this space for more simple tools to help you sound fluent in jargon and decipher frequently used phrases.
